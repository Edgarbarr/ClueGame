This Clue is a text-based murder mystery. You joined some friends for dinner then suddenly one of them winds up murdered. Who did it? And with what Weapon?You begin in a randomly assigned room.You can view the room and are given two clues.You should take note of the clues – the mystery is solved by deduction.Next you can choose to make a guess about the killer and the murder weapon or leave the room.Each room has openings only to the adjacent rooms.Press 3 to see a list of CharactersPress 4 to see a list of WeaponsPress 5 to see a list of RoomsAt anytime you can quit by pressing 0Press 2 to Play the Clue!
